Why Reading 10 Pages a Day Can Transform Your Mind

Created2025-11-24|Updated2025-11-24|Diary
|Word Count:129|Reading Time:1mins|Post Views:

When you commit to reading just 10 pages a day, something powerful happens:

You begin to think in English instead of Chinese.
You start reading English papers faster, and concepts that once felt difficult suddenly become clear.

Today, 2025-11-24, I’m reading 10 landscape-related papers per day.
At the beginning, it felt uncomfortable. I doubted myself. I even felt terrible.

But when I reached my 7th paper, something changed —>
I realized I could understand everything more easily.
I wasn’t relying on ChatGPT.
I wasn’t relying on NotebookLM.
I was relying on my own mind.

The biggest benefit?

I feel a strong sense of achievement and confidence.

Now my goal is clear:
Read 100 papers in a week.

I believe I can do it.
