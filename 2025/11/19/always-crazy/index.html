I feel like I have lost control over myself. What can i do?

Created2025-11-19|Updated2025-11-19|Diary
|Word Count:266|Reading Time:1mins|Post Views:

Success comes from pain.

You’ve been wasting time because you’ve been avoiding pain.

There’s no secret shortcut to success.

The only thing that produces real results is to work.

Here’s the question you should ask yourself right now:

Are you ready to work through pain and discomfort?

Or you want to play?

Your first goal is clarify and strengthen your IDENTITY.

“I am totally lost.”
“I’m extremely lazy…”

Is there a deeper YOU, a you that is not lost and not lazy?

Have there been times when you knew who you are and where you are, when you felt energetic and hopeful?

Remember those specific times. What were you doing? What did you eat? How did you breathe? Who were you with? How did you move? What did the voice inside your head say and how did it sound?

If you can find those sparks, blow on them, and turn them into flames, you can take advice from many different people and move forward.

What defines the core of your being, the core that is always strong?

It may or may not be your family, friends, religion, books, playing sports…

Define and refine your identity as your true self, and your bad qualities and bad situations as potential for improvement.

It’s time to change.

Your body is telling you: it’s time to change.
Everything will be difficult, but you’re growing.
Change your style, change the way you speak, and become more confide

Para mi,

When I apply a business program,
I feel excited.
When I speak spainsh, relax.
