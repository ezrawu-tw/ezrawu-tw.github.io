Letter from +7

2026.05.07 she said

What I want to say is Every time I see you convert your ideals into action, it drives me forward. It’s amazing. We obviously don’t contact each other very often. So you have always been a friend that I am very proud of. You will attract equally good people as you said in your writing. Like me :D)

I cried. I miss her so much. My fifth best friend and bestie

There is always room for you in my heart

It’s a bit like being in parallel time and space, right? the feeling of your name

I still remember the 19th birthday gift you gave me That hand drawing of yours is engraved in my mind
