# Learning Takes Patience
In Jordan's teaching videos,
the last slide mentions
that the key to learning is patience.

Teacher Jordan told me:
When I first started on 03.05.26,
I could only understand 20–30% of what you said.
But now I can understand 65–75% of what you say.

I'm so happy about this.
It turns out that progress is something you can't see yourself — but the people around you see it very clearly.
