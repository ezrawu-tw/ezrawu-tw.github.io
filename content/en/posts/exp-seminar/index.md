You can find past papers from a conference you're interested in, right-click and print the page as a PDF for a bunch of paper titles, then give it to Claude to see if any match your topic.
You can also ask Perplexity to search for related ones.
Otherwise, look for papers with similar problem statements.

You can first use Gemini to extract summary keywords —
10 from an engineering perspective and 10 from a design perspective, then organize the most important ones.
After the analysis, give it the full text for a more accurate result.
For the filtered papers, you don't have to translate them one by one yourself.
